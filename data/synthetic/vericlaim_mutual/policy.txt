DEFINITIONS
================================================================================

CLAUSE_ID: DEFINITIONS

Throughout this policy, the following words have the meanings set forth below.

"You" and "your" refer to the Named Insured shown in the Declarations and the
Named Insured's spouse, if a resident of the same household.

"We", "us" and "our" refer to VeriClaim Mutual Insurance Company.

"Bodily injury" means bodily harm, sickness or disease, including death that
results from such bodily harm, sickness or disease.

"Collision" means the upset of your covered auto or a non-owned auto or their
impact with another vehicle or object.

"Covered auto" means any vehicle shown in the Declarations for which a premium
charge is indicated, including a newly acquired auto, a trailer you own, and a
temporary substitute auto.

"Deductible" means the amount you must pay before we cover a loss. Your
deductible is shown in the Declarations.

"Loss" means direct and accidental loss or damage to a covered auto or
non-owned auto, including their equipment.

"Market value" means the amount a willing buyer would pay a willing seller for
the covered auto in an arm's length transaction at the time of the loss.

"Non-owned auto" means any private passenger auto, pickup, van or trailer not
owned by or furnished or made available for regular use of you or any family
member.

"Occupying" means in, upon, getting in, on, out or off.

"Property damage" means physical injury to, destruction of, or loss of use of
tangible property.

"Total loss" means damage to the covered auto where repair costs plus salvage
value equal or exceed the actual cash value of the covered auto. We determine
a total loss when estimated repair costs exceed 75% of the pre-loss market
value of the vehicle.

"Uninsured motor vehicle" means a land motor vehicle for which no liability
bond or policy applies at the time of the accident.

PART A — LIABILITY COVERAGE
================================================================================

CLAUSE_ID: PART_A.1
SECTION: PART A — LIABILITY COVERAGE
SUBSECTION: 1. Insuring Agreement

We will pay damages for bodily injury or property damage for which any covered
person becomes legally responsible because of an auto accident. We will settle
or defend, at our option, any claim or suit asking for these damages. Our duty
to settle or defend ends when our limit of liability for this coverage has been
exhausted by payment of settlements or judgments.

CLAUSE_ID: PART_A.2
SECTION: PART A — LIABILITY COVERAGE
SUBSECTION: 2. Exclusions — Liability Coverage

We do not provide Liability Coverage for any person:

a) Who intentionally causes bodily injury or property damage.

b) For property damage to property owned or being transported by that person.

c) For bodily injury to an employee of that person during the course of
employment (covered under Workers' Compensation).

d) For that person's liability arising out of the ownership or operation of a
vehicle while it is being used as a public or livery conveyance (rideshare
exclusion applies — see Endorsement VC-003 for rideshare coverage options).

e) While operating a vehicle without a valid driver's license.

CLAUSE_ID: PART_A.3
SECTION: PART A — LIABILITY COVERAGE
SUBSECTION: 3. Limit of Liability

The limit of liability shown in the Declarations for each person is the maximum
we will pay for damages for bodily injury, including all derivative claims,
sustained by any one person in any one accident. The limit of liability for
each accident is the maximum we will pay for all damages resulting from one
accident. These limits apply regardless of the number of covered autos, insured
persons, claims made, vehicles involved, or suits brought.

================================================================================
PART B — MEDICAL PAYMENTS COVERAGE
================================================================================

CLAUSE_ID: PART_B.1
SECTION: PART B — MEDICAL PAYMENTS COVERAGE
SUBSECTION: 1. Insuring Agreement

We will pay reasonable expenses incurred for necessary medical and funeral
services because of bodily injury caused by accident and sustained by a covered
person. We will pay only those expenses incurred for services rendered within
three years from the date of the accident. The limit of liability for this
coverage is shown in the Declarations.

CLAUSE_ID: PART_B.2
SECTION: PART B — MEDICAL PAYMENTS COVERAGE
SUBSECTION: 2. Exclusions — Medical Payments

We do not provide Medical Payments Coverage for any person for bodily injury:

a) Sustained while occupying any motorized vehicle having fewer than four
wheels.

b) Sustained while occupying your covered auto when it is being used as a
public or livery conveyance.

c) Sustained while occupying a vehicle without a reasonable belief of
entitlement.

================================================================================
PART C — UNINSURED MOTORISTS COVERAGE
================================================================================

CLAUSE_ID: PART_C.1
SECTION: PART C — UNINSURED MOTORISTS COVERAGE
SUBSECTION: 1. Insuring Agreement

We will pay compensatory damages which a covered person is legally entitled to
recover from the owner or operator of an uninsured motor vehicle because of
bodily injury sustained by a covered person and caused by an accident. The
owner's or operator's liability for these damages must arise out of the
ownership, maintenance or use of an uninsured motor vehicle.

CLAUSE_ID: PART_C.2
SECTION: PART C — UNINSURED MOTORISTS COVERAGE
SUBSECTION: 2. Limit of Liability

Our maximum payment for all damages resulting from any one accident is the
lesser of: (a) the difference between the amount of damages for bodily injury
and the amount paid or payable by or on behalf of persons or organizations who
may be legally responsible for the bodily injury; and (b) the limit of
liability for this coverage.

PART D — COVERAGE FOR DAMAGE TO YOUR AUTO
================================================================================

CLAUSE_ID: PART_D.1
SECTION: PART D — COVERAGE FOR DAMAGE TO YOUR AUTO
SUBSECTION: 1. Insuring Agreement

a) Collision Coverage: We will pay for direct and accidental loss to your
covered auto or any non-owned auto, minus any applicable deductible shown in
the Declarations, if the loss results from collision.

b) Other Than Collision (Comprehensive) Coverage: We will pay for direct and
accidental loss to your covered auto or any non-owned auto, minus any
applicable deductible shown in the Declarations, if the loss does not result
from collision. Losses covered under Other Than Collision include: theft,
windstorm, hail, fire, explosion, earthquake, flood, vandalism, falling
objects, and contact with bird or animal.

c) For a loss caused by collision, the Collision Deductible applies.
For a loss caused by a peril covered under Other Than Collision, the
Other Than Collision Deductible applies. Both deductibles are shown in the
Declarations.

CLAUSE_ID: PART_D.2
SECTION: PART D — COVERAGE FOR DAMAGE TO YOUR AUTO
SUBSECTION: 2. Transportation Expenses

In addition to our limit of liability, we will pay up to $30 per day and up to
a maximum of $900 for transportation expenses incurred by you because of loss
to a covered auto. Transportation expenses are only payable when the covered
auto is:
a) Stolen and not recovered within 48 hours of the theft; or
b) Damaged in a covered loss and in the shop for repair.
Payment begins 48 hours after the theft or the date the covered auto is
delivered to the repair shop, whichever applies.

CLAUSE_ID: PART_D.3
SECTION: PART D — COVERAGE FOR DAMAGE TO YOUR AUTO
SUBSECTION: 3. Exclusions — Damage to Your Auto

We will not pay for loss to a covered auto or non-owned auto caused by or
resulting from any of the following:

a) Wear and tear, freezing, mechanical or electrical breakdown or failure,
road damage to tires. This exclusion does not apply if the damage results from
the total theft of your covered auto.

b) Radioactive contamination or the explosion of nuclear weapons.

c) Destruction or confiscation by governmental or civil authorities.

d) Racing — loss while the vehicle is used in any racing contest, speed
contest, hill-climbing contest, jumping contest, or any similar contest.

e) Electronic equipment not permanently installed in the covered auto,
including communication devices, data systems, and media players.

f) Awnings, cabanas, or equipment designed to create additional living
facilities, unless permanently installed.

g) Diminution in value — any reduction in market value of the covered auto
after it has been repaired.

h) Custom parts or equipment unless described in the Declarations or covered
by Endorsement VC-004.

i) Loss to a non-owned auto used without a reasonable belief of entitlement.

CLAUSE_ID: PART_D.4
SECTION: PART D — COVERAGE FOR DAMAGE TO YOUR AUTO
SUBSECTION: 4. Limit of Liability

a) Our limit of liability for loss is the lesser of:
   (1) The actual cash value of the stolen or damaged property; or
   (2) The amount necessary to repair or replace the property with other
       property of like kind and quality.

b) An adjustment for depreciation and physical condition will be made in
determining actual cash value in the event of a total loss.

c) If a repair or replacement results in better than like kind or quality,
we will not pay for the amount of betterment.

d) For total loss (as defined herein), we will pay the actual cash value of
the covered auto at the time of the loss, minus your applicable deductible.
We reserve the right to offer a replacement vehicle of like kind and quality
in lieu of a cash settlement.

e) For partial loss (repairs), we will pay the cost of repair minus your
applicable deductible, provided that repairs restore the vehicle to its
pre-loss condition.

CLAUSE_ID: PART_D.5
SECTION: PART D — COVERAGE FOR DAMAGE TO YOUR AUTO
SUBSECTION: 5. Payment of Loss — Deductible Application

a) We may pay for loss in money or repair or replace the damaged or stolen
property. We may, at our expense, return any stolen property to you or pay for
the loss. If we return stolen property, we will pay for any damage resulting
from the theft. We may keep all or part of the property at the agreed or
appraised value.

b) The applicable deductible will be waived if: (i) a clearly identified,
uninsured motorist is at fault for the collision; and (ii) you report the
accident to law enforcement within 24 hours.

c) Your deductible applies per occurrence, not per damaged component.

PART E — DUTIES AFTER AN ACCIDENT OR LOSS
================================================================================

CLAUSE_ID: PART_E.1
SECTION: PART E — DUTIES AFTER AN ACCIDENT OR LOSS
SUBSECTION: 1. General Duties

We must be notified promptly of how, when and where the accident or loss
happened. Notice should include the names and addresses of any injured persons
and any witnesses.

A person seeking coverage must:
a) Cooperate with us in the investigation, settlement or defense of any claim
or suit.

b) Promptly send us copies of any notices or legal papers received in
connection with the accident or loss.

c) Submit to physical exams by physicians we select as often as we reasonably
require.

d) Authorize us to obtain medical reports and other pertinent records.

e) Submit a proof of loss when required by us.

CLAUSE_ID: PART_E.2
SECTION: PART E — DUTIES AFTER AN ACCIDENT OR LOSS
SUBSECTION: 2. Additional Duties for Coverage for Damage to Your Auto

A person seeking Coverage for Damage to Your Auto must also:
a) Take reasonable steps after loss to protect the covered auto from further
loss. We will pay reasonable expenses incurred to do this.

b) Promptly notify the police if the covered auto is stolen.

c) Permit us to inspect and appraise the damaged property before its repair
or disposal.

d) Submit to examination under oath as required.

PART F — GENERAL PROVISIONS
================================================================================

CLAUSE_ID: PART_F.1
SECTION: PART F — GENERAL PROVISIONS
SUBSECTION: 1. Policy Period and Territory

This policy applies only to accidents and losses which occur during the policy
period as shown in the Declarations, and within the United States of America,
its territories or possessions, Puerto Rico or Canada.

CLAUSE_ID: PART_F.2
SECTION: PART F — GENERAL PROVISIONS
SUBSECTION: 2. Fraud

If any person seeking coverage under this policy has made fraudulent statements
or engaged in fraudulent conduct in connection with any accident or loss for
which coverage is sought, coverage is void and we will not pay any such claim.
We reserve the right to recover any amounts paid on such fraudulent claims.
Fraudulent indicators include but are not limited to: staged accidents,
inflated repair estimates, misrepresentation of vehicle condition, and
submission of false documentation.

CLAUSE_ID: PART_F.3
SECTION: PART F — GENERAL PROVISIONS
SUBSECTION: 3. Our Right to Recover Payment

If we make a payment under this policy and the person to or for whom payment
was made has a right to recover damages from another, we will be subrogated to
that right. That person shall do everything necessary to secure our rights and
shall do nothing after loss to prejudice them.

CLAUSE_ID: PART_F.4
SECTION: PART F — GENERAL PROVISIONS
SUBSECTION: 4. Changes

This policy contains all the agreements between you and us. Its terms may not
be changed or waived except by endorsement issued by us. If a change requires
a premium adjustment, we will adjust the premium as of the effective date of
change.

CLAUSE_ID: PART_F.5
SECTION: PART F — GENERAL PROVISIONS
SUBSECTION: 5. Termination

a) Cancellation: This policy may be cancelled during the policy period as
follows: (i) You may cancel at any time by returning this policy or by giving
us advance written notice. (ii) We may cancel by mailing to the Named Insured
at least 10 days notice of cancellation before cancellation is effective for
nonpayment of premium, or at least 30 days notice for any other reason.

b) Non-renewal: If we decide not to renew or continue this policy, we will
mail notice to the Named Insured at least 30 days before the end of the
policy period.
