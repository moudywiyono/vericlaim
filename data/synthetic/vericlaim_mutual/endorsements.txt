VERICLAIM MUTUAL INSURANCE COMPANY
POLICY ENDORSEMENTS
Form VC-ENDORSE-2024

These endorsements modify insurance provided under:
AUTOMOBILE INSURANCE POLICY — Form VC-AUTO-2024

================================================================================
ENDORSEMENT VC-001 — RENTAL REIMBURSEMENT COVERAGE
================================================================================

CLAUSE_ID: ENDORSEMENT_VC001.1
ENDORSEMENT: VC-001
MODIFIES: PART_D.2
SUBSECTION: Coverage Grant

With respect to a covered auto for which this endorsement applies, we will pay
reasonable rental charges you incur because your covered auto is not driveable
or is being repaired as a result of a covered loss. Coverage begins on the
date of the covered loss. We will pay up to $50 per day, up to a maximum of
$1,500 per loss occurrence.

CLAUSE_ID: ENDORSEMENT_VC001.2
ENDORSEMENT: VC-001
MODIFIES: PART_D.2
SUBSECTION: Conditions

Rental reimbursement does not apply to theft of the covered auto unless the
vehicle is not recovered within 48 hours. Coverage terminates on the earliest
of: (a) the date repair of the covered auto is completed; (b) the date we
offer settlement for a total loss; or (c) the date coverage limits are
exhausted.

================================================================================
ENDORSEMENT VC-002 — LOAN/LEASE GAP COVERAGE
================================================================================

CLAUSE_ID: ENDORSEMENT_VC002.1
ENDORSEMENT: VC-002
MODIFIES: PART_D.4
SUBSECTION: Coverage Grant

In the event of a total loss to a covered auto, we will pay, in addition to
the actual cash value payment under PART D, the difference between:
a) The actual cash value of the covered auto at the time of the loss; and
b) The outstanding balance of the loan or lease on the covered auto at the
time of the loss.

The maximum additional payment under this endorsement is limited to 25% of
the actual cash value of the covered auto.

CLAUSE_ID: ENDORSEMENT_VC002.2
ENDORSEMENT: VC-002
MODIFIES: PART_D.4
SUBSECTION: Exclusions

Gap coverage does not apply to: (a) amounts past due or deferred at the time
of the loss; (b) lease charges such as security deposits, overdue payments,
or financial penalties; (c) carry-over balances from previous loans or leases;
or (d) any amount rolled into the current loan from a prior transaction.

================================================================================
ENDORSEMENT VC-003 — RIDESHARE COVERAGE EXTENSION
================================================================================

CLAUSE_ID: ENDORSEMENT_VC003.1
ENDORSEMENT: VC-003
MODIFIES: PART_A.2.d
SUBSECTION: Coverage Extension

Notwithstanding the livery/rideshare exclusion in PART A, Section 2(d), we
will provide liability coverage during Period 1 of rideshare activity
(app on, waiting for a match) for covered autos used in connection with a
Transportation Network Company (TNC). Coverage during Period 2 (match
accepted) and Period 3 (passenger in vehicle) is the responsibility of the
TNC's commercial policy, and this endorsement does not extend coverage to
those periods unless specifically stated in the Declarations.

CLAUSE_ID: ENDORSEMENT_VC003.2
ENDORSEMENT: VC-003
MODIFIES: PART_D.1
SUBSECTION: Physical Damage Extension

Physical damage coverage (PART D) extends to covered losses occurring during
Period 1 of rideshare activity. A $500 rideshare deductible applies in lieu
of the standard deductible for losses occurring during Period 1. Coverage does
not extend to Periods 2 or 3.

================================================================================
ENDORSEMENT VC-004 — CUSTOM PARTS AND EQUIPMENT
================================================================================

CLAUSE_ID: ENDORSEMENT_VC004.1
ENDORSEMENT: VC-004
MODIFIES: PART_D.3.h
SUBSECTION: Coverage Grant

Notwithstanding the exclusion in PART D, Section 3(h), we will pay for loss
to custom parts or equipment installed in or upon the covered auto, up to the
limit shown in the Declarations for this endorsement (maximum $5,000). Custom
parts and equipment means equipment, devices, accessories, enhancements, and
changes, other than those which are original manufacturer equipment, that
alter the appearance or performance of a vehicle.

CLAUSE_ID: ENDORSEMENT_VC004.2
ENDORSEMENT: VC-004
MODIFIES: PART_D.3.h
SUBSECTION: Conditions

Coverage under this endorsement applies only to custom parts and equipment
that are permanently installed and specifically described in the schedule
attached to this endorsement. Aftermarket audio equipment is covered only if
permanently installed in the dashboard or factory location.
